About

My primary field of interest is Human-Computer Interaction, and I am passionate about Recommendation Systems, especially their security and privacy aspects. Additionally, I am enthusiastic about exploring the potential of AR/VR technologies for enhancing the field of Computer0Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW).
